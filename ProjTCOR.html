#ProjectTCOR

The time was 8:00am, the venue - THE REDEEMED CHRISTIAN CHURCH OF GOD, CITY OF REFUGE, the sun taking its place in the sky as praises rose to the heavens and worship in honor of God. As usual, members strolled in to join in praising God for His deeds...but something was different. What was different? There was a display of different items up for grabs and there was a doctor on sit. FEEDING GATE FOUNDATION was given a warm welcome by the Pastor Joseph Ekhator and the welfare Department of THE CITY OF REFUGE. The pastorate, Welfare department and Feeding gate Foundation were kind enough to provide food items, clothing items as well as volunteers to help in the smooth running of the event. People went for medical check-up first before going in to listen to the word of God has delivered by Pastor Amos Emovon. THE CITY OF REFUGE auditorium was filled to the brim...and there was even an overflow of both people and blessings as the people enjoyed the message, which was followed by intense prayers. The service was over and then came the outflow; an outflow of excited people who could not afford the heavy bills of hospitals but were going to be treated and take free drugs, food items and even clothing items. The ushers of THE CITY OF REFUGE were as kind as to volunteering as well to see to it that the programme went according to plan. The joy written all over the faces of Nigerians who were present made the atmosphere warm and homely as the beautiful faces shone brightly with HOPE. FEEDING GATE FOUNDATION was given an opportunity once again to lighten up the faces of those present at THE CITY OF REFUGE. A BIG "THANK YOU" to Almighty GOD for the privilege.
