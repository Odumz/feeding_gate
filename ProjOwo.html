#ProjectOworonsoki

Just like every other day, the sun rose over the day to make it a complete day except that it was October 1st. Independence day is usually celebrated in Nigeria to commemorate the struggle by past Nigerian leaders for our territory to make decisions on our own as well as have a sovereign government which would take care of official obligations of the territory called NIGERIA. October 1st also happened to be the day the beautiful settlement of OWORONSHOKI decided to welcome THE FEEDING GATE FOUNDATION to their environment. The sight of the amazing people of that settlement cut out hearts deep as these are people that amidst the situation of their lives..they live a happy life. They welcomed us with opened arms...they were eager to be treated,the kids beemed with excitment at the thought of carrying independence flags. They beemed and mentioned Nigeria with pride. If only the government could see this community who are so proud of this country. The old women lingered and did not want to be left out of the excitement,They wanted to be involved even the men too. As the sound of music filled the air I was amazed to see talent...raw and amazing talent in this settlement...Children danced like professionals...I stood in awe looking amazed at what these kids exhibited. Feeding gate foundation was proud to be there to bring Christmas to Oworonshoki in October...
