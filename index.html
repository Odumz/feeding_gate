Welcome To Feeding Gate Foundation

Feeding Gate Foundation is an NGO established for the African people to be constructive partners in the renewed effort to meet the needs of the poor and destitute through life-changing programs focused on food, health and empowerment. A sole means to pin-point our common weaknesses as well as unite our strength in both the short and long run, keeping close relationship with local and international development partners of similar aspiration.

Our vision is to eradicate extreme hunger and starvation through our social services and community based initiatives. We provide for the needy in our communities, villages and in cities where hunger and starvation is raging. Our view of the charity world is that compared with business, there is too much talk, way too many meetings and expert panels and blue-ribbon commissions, and not enough action. A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step; act accordingly!

What We Do

Children Support

A child today, a leader tomorrow! You can make a longlasting impact in a child's life by helping provide food, clothing, education e.t.c. Every child deserves an equal opportunity.

Youth Development

Research has shown that a young person will be three times more likely to be unemployed and this is one of the biggest economic problems. Feeding Gate Foundation is a local, Nigerian-run NGO that is aimed at engaging, encouraging and providing necessary skills needed for the job market. We support active youth, creativity, art, entrepreneurship and culture.

Women Empowerment

Women represent nearly two thirds of the world’s illiterate. We believe that by empowering women, we can empower whole communities. Women hold the key to educating their children, the next generation who will go on to shape the future of Nigeria. According to UNICEF, girls' education literally saves millions of lives.

Community Development

Charity they say, begins at home. By providing for the children as well as empowering the youth and women, we are providing a better quality of life for everyone at large.
