Why Donate?

Feeding Gate Foundation was registered in 2016. It was established to meet the poor and needy.

Our vision is to eradicate extreme hunger and starvation through our social services and community based initiatives. We provide for the needy in our communities, villages and in cities where hunger and starvation is raging.
